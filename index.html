How to Play 67 Clicker Game

Clicking, Upgrading, and Passive Income

At its heart, the 67 Clicker operates on simple, yet profound, economic principles. The entire game revolves around managing the flow of your Resources (67s) to achieve maximum output.

Clicking

Begin your journey by constantly Clicking the Main Icon, the large Number "67" displayed prominently on the screen. Each click generates a small number of Base Points.

Upgrading

Use the earned 67s to purchase Upgrade Types and unlock automated systems. Your first critical decision is often the Early Automation Rush, prioritizing initial passive income generators like the Cursor, Auto, and Farm.

Various Upgrade Types

Cursor: Your foundational upgrade, boosting the output of every manual Clicking action. Essential for generating the initial capital needed for automation.

Auto and Farm: These are your starting points for Early Automation Rush, providing reliable passive income that frees you from non-stop clicking.

67 Man: A core automated tool, the 67 Man provides a steady, high-tier stream of 67s, serving as a critical mid-game investment.

67 Rocket: The ultimate automation tool. Investing in the 67 Rocket often marks the transition into true Exponential Growth, generating colossal amounts of currency and pushing you toward the highest Milestone Challenges.

Mastering Exponential Growth

Success in the 67 Clicker hinges on exploiting the Exponential Growth Mechanics. Every new purchase contributes to a faster rate of gain, ensuring your score rises with increasing speed.

By intelligently switching between manual Clicking (for immediate cash) and automated investment, you can push your point generation to dizzying heights, asserting your dominance in this definitive Idle Game.
